## How to Authenticate Charizard Cards

As the demand for Charizard cards increases, so does the prevalence of counterfeit cards. Here are some tips for authenticating them:

- **Card Quality**: Authentic cards have high print quality with vibrant colors and sharp details.
- **Card Thickness**: Genuine cards have consistent thickness and a moderate feel.
- **Light Test**: Hold the card against a light source; authentic cards often exhibit unique light-transmitting effects.
