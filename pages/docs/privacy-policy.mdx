# Privacy Policy for AutoFormAI

## Introduction

Welcome to AutoFormAI! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our Chrome extension. We are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of your data. By using AutoFormAI, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

AutoFormAI does **not** collect, share, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All your data, including your personal information library, is stored **locally** on your device and remains private.

### Types of Data Stored Locally:

- Personal Information Library: The information you store locally to automatically fill out forms (e.g., names, addresses, emails, etc.).
- Preferences and Settings: Custom configurations and preferences related to the extension’s operation.

## How We Use Your Information

AutoFormAI utilizes the locally stored data to provide the following services:

- **Form Autofill**: We use your personal information library to automatically fill out web forms on your behalf.
- **Customization**: Based on your preferences, we may store custom settings locally to improve your user experience.

### No Data Sharing:
AutoFormAI does not send your personal data to any third-party services, nor do we upload your information to external servers. Your personal information is stored securely within your device’s local storage.

## Security

We are committed to ensuring that your personal information is secure. We take appropriate measures to safeguard the data stored locally on your device, and no external access to your data is allowed.

However, it's important to note that you are responsible for maintaining the security of your device. Clearing browser cache or uninstalling the extension could result in data loss.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last Updated" date will be updated accordingly. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.
